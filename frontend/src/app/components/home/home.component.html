Cultural Profile Matcher

What is it?

A good potential worker is not only the one who has the best skills, it is also necessary that he or she has cultural values compatible with the business and the specific job. This is what Cultural Profile Matcher want to show to users and companies. This application allows to calculate a compatibility factor of the cultural values a person and a job, compatibility factor of the cultural, improving the selection process of the business.

4

You can also visualize the cultural profile computed for the selected user.

How do we do it?

After we have obtained the user and job cultural profiles we consider the polygons generated by the radar plots of them. Then, we use Montecarlo mthods to calculate the area of the union and the intersection of these polygons. Finally, whe calculate the ratio of the union and intersection areas to provide you a compatibility factor of the cultural profile of your job and the candidate you are evaluting.
