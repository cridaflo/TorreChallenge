Cultural Profile Matcher

What is it?

A good potential worker is not only the one who has the best skills, it is also necessary that he/she has cultural values compatible with the business and the specific job. This is what Cultural Profile Matcher wants to show to users and companies. This application allows to calculate a cultural compatibility factor between an applicant and a job, improving business' recruiting process.

How to use it?

1

Select the five professional dynamics that you consider more important.

Take into account the company's cultural values and the requirements for the job. We use this to calculate the cultural profile of your job.

2

Visualize the generated cultural profile.

We will use later the polygon that you can see in this chart.

3

Search the user you want to evaluate.

We use the information from the professional culture test of the user's genome in order to calculate his/her cultural profile.

4

Get your compatibility score!

You can also visualize the cultural profile computed for the selected user and the job.

How do we do it?

After we obtain the user and job cultural profiles, we consider the polygons generated by their radar plots. Then, we use Montecarlo methods to calculate the union and intersection areas of these polygons. Finally, we calculate the union and intersection areas ratio to provide you the compatibility factor.
